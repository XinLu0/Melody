Melody Strings Art Center - Orchestra Training Programme
To compliment our teaching method and further allow our musicians to develop the 5 abilities, Melody Strings Chamber Orchestra was established in 2015. Each orchestra member (aged between 5-17) is selected through a rigorous audition process.
  1. The success of our teaching method can be seen through the achievements of Melody Strings Chamber Orchestra. To
date, they have obtained 5 Gold Awards in international music competitions.
  1. From March 2017, we are proud to say that our orchestra training programme has officially moved to Victoria Concert
Hall - Music Studio.
  1. Our outstanding conductors are from Singapore Symphony Orchestra.

  2. Every year, Melody Strings Art Center will provide numerous performance opportunities for our orchestra.

  3. Our musicians have also obtained a total of 50 distinction awards in the ABRSM grade examination who will receive a
school cash voucher SGD$50 that you may spend it on goods purchased at our school.
